Privacy Policy

This page explains what personal data this website processes, why, and what rights you have. Last updated 17 July 2026.

Who we are

Fortis Anima Consulting, Stationsvägen 16, 693 73 Svartå, Sweden (organisation number 19840204-5499), is the data controller for this website. Contact: info@fortis-anima.com.

What data we process

This site does not use cookies, analytics or tracking scripts, and has no contact form that stores submissions. The only personal data involved is:

Server logs. Our hosting provider (Vercel Inc.) automatically records technical data for every visit — such as IP address, browser type and request timestamps — to operate and secure the site. We don't access this data for marketing purposes.

Third-party icon library. This site loads an icon library from unpkg.com. Loading it transmits your IP address to that third-party service, the same way it would for any external resource.

Email enquiries. If you email us, we process whatever personal data you include (e.g. your name, email address and message) to respond to your enquiry.

Why we process it

Server logs are processed on the basis of our legitimate interest in keeping the site secure and running reliably (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)). Email enquiries are processed to respond to you and, where relevant, to take steps towards a consultation you've requested (Art. 6(1)(b)/(f)).

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your personal data, to object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these, email info@fortis-anima.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY), imy.se.

Changes to this policy

If how this site processes personal data changes — for example if we add analytics or a contact form — we'll update this page accordingly.
